Construction Law

Contact Us

Milton Advocates has deep expertise in construction and infrastructure law across Uganda and the East African region. Our practice covers the full project lifecycle, providing clients with strategic legal support at every stage — from contract formation and procurement through to claims management and dispute resolution. We advise on:

  • FIDIC, NEC, JCT and bespoke construction contracts
  • Procurement and tender advisory
  • Claims preparation and management
  • Adjudication and dispute boards
  • PPP, EPC and BOOT project structures
  • Construction insurance and bonds

Arbitration

Contact Us

With specialist qualifications in construction law and dispute resolution and active membership of leading arbitral institutions, Milton Advocates provides authoritative representation in domestic and international proceedings. We act as counsel, arbitrator and adviser across:

  • ICC, LCIA, UNCITRAL and institutional arbitration
  • CADER and domestic tribunal proceedings
  • Emergency arbitration and interim relief
  • Arbitration agreement drafting
  • Award enforcement and challenge proceedings
  • Expert determination and dispute adjudication boards

Energy Law

Contact Us

As Uganda’s energy sector continues to expand, Milton Advocates provides authoritative legal advice to developers, contractors, investors and government entities. Our energy practice combines deep sector knowledge with expertise in project structures and regulatory frameworks. We advise on:

  • Energy project finance and security arrangements
  • Dispute resolution for energy and infrastructure projects

Tax Law

Contact Us

Milton Advocates provides strategic tax advice and representation to businesses, investors and individuals operating in Uganda. We help clients understand their obligations under Ugandan tax law, structure their affairs efficiently and, where necessary, defend their positions before the Uganda Revenue Authority and the courts. Our tax practice covers:

  • Tax compliance, statutory filings and returns

  • Tax appeals before the Tax Appeals Tribunal and courts
  • Transfer pricing and international tax advisory
  • VAT, income tax, withholding tax and stamp duty advice

Milton Ocen

Senior Partner & CEO

MSc Construction Law & Dispute Resolution, British University in Dubai · LLB (MUK) · Dip LP(LDC) · MCIArb · LCIA Member

Milton F. Ocen is the Managing Partner at Milton Advocates, a firm specialising in Construction Law, Arbitration, Commercial Transactions, and Litigation. He holds an MSc in Construction Law & Dispute Resolution from the British University in Dubai and an LLB from Makerere University.

With over 16 years of experience, he is highly skilled in dispute resolution involving FIDIC Contracts, ICC Rules, UNCITRAL Rules, and other major international arbitration frameworks.

He has acted as counsel and arbitrator in key infrastructure and construction projects across Uganda and internationally, offering expert legal advice on project management, contract administration, and project financing options such as PPP, EPC, and BOOT.

His extensive involvement with CADER, LCIA, and the National Building Review Board demonstrates his leadership in shaping Uganda’s construction law landscape. He is an accredited mediator by Pepperdine University.
