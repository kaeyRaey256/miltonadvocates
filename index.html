Milton Ocen

Senior Partner

MSc in Construction Law & Dispute Resolution, University in Dubai, LLB (MUK), Dip LP(LDC)

Milton F. Ocen is the Managing Partner at Milton Advocates, a firm specialising in Construction Law, Arbitration, Commercial Transactions, and Litigation. He holds an MSc in Construction Law & Dispute Resolution from the British University in Dubai and an LLB from Makerere University. With over 16 years of experience.

He is highly skilled in dispute resolution involving FIDIC Contracts, ICC Rules, UNCITRAL Rules, and other major international arbitration frameworks.

He has acted as counsel and arbitrator in key infrastructure and construction projects across Uganda and internationally, offering expert legal advice on project management, contract administration, and project financing options such as PPP, EPC, and BOOT. His extensive involvement with CADER, LCIA, and the National Building Review Board demonstrates his leadership in shaping Uganda's construction law landscape.

Certifications include MCIArb, member of LCIA, and accredited mediator by Pepperdine Universi
